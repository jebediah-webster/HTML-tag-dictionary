html Tag Dictionary



a:defines beginning and end of hyperlink texts

attributes: href, download, target, title, name, & hreflang

example:<a>a website<a>



h1: header tag, makes the text large and puts it on a separate line

attributes: none

example:<h1>Mr pelletier</h1>


h6:defines least important heading

attributes:none

example:<h6>mr pelletier<h6>



img:defines an image

attributes:src & alt

example:<img src="smiley.gif" alt="Smiley face" height="42" width="42">



li:tag placed within either <ol> tag or <ul> tag to represent each individual item on the list

attributes:none

example:<ul>
	<li>item</li>
	<li>item</li>
	<li>item</li>
</ul>



ol:defines an ordered list

attributes:none

example:<ol>
  <li>the first day of christmas</li>
  <li>the second day of christmas</li>
  <li>the third day of christmas</li>
  <li>the fourth day of christmas</li>
  <li>the fifth day of christmas</li>
</ol>



p:defines a paragraph

attributes:none

example:<p>paragraph text</p>



ul: defines an unordered list

attributes:none

example:<ul>
	<li>item</li>
	<li>item</li>
	<li>item</li>
</ul>
